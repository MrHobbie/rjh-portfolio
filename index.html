ABOUT ME

Digital Content Producer

Radio, live music and the entertainment industry have always been a passion of mine. It lead me to pursuing my post secondary education of Broadcasting, Performing Arts and English.

For the past 10 years my professional career has been in the digital space for radio. My position is to reflect the station online. I’ve worked with various radio formats including Alternative Rock to News Talk to Pop Music. Each has come with their own brand of challenges but always plenty of excitement. Over these 10 years I’ve watched the explosion of social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter & Instagram, combined with the popularity of videos led by YouTube. With these new platforms and our radio audiences spending increasing amounts of time here it has been important for myself to be on top of these trends and make sure our radio brands are positioned where our listeners are.

In addition to mirroring the stations online and in social media, it has been increasingly important to build in client solutions. Just like you would hear a commercial on your car radio, it has been my role to build out client driven promotions online. I’d encourage you to view some of these in my portfolio section.

I consider myself lucky to have spent the majority of my career in not only “the never say die” radio business but the constant evolving digital platform.
